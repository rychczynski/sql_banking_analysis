1. Credit Limit by Card Brand
Question: What is the average credit limit for each card brand?
Insight: Visa leads the market with the highest average limits. While Mastercard has a similar volume of cards, Visa remains the top choice for high-limit credit products in this dataset.

2. Customer Loyalty vs. Credit Limit
Question: Do customers with multiple cards have higher limits?
Insight: Surprisingly, Single-card holders have a significantly higher average limit ($15,308) than Multi-card holders ($13,360). This suggests that the highest credit limits are assigned to primary, single-card accounts rather than being spread across multiple products.

3. Security Gap: Chip Adoption
Question: How many cards do not have a security chip and what is their percentage?
Insight: I identified that 11% of the cards still lack an EMV chip. This represents a clear physical security gap that could be addressed to reduce fraud risk.
